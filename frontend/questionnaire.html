Document Integrity Research Intake

Complete this short questionnaire to access the Forgensic console. Your answers help us improve model quality, safety, and deployment readiness.

1) Purpose

We collect non-identifying operational details to understand how document forgery detection is used across industries and regions. This helps us prioritize model improvements and reporting.

2) What You Will Do

Answer a short set of questions about your organization and document types. This takes about 2-3 minutes.

3) Data & Privacy

No personal identifiers are required. Do not upload sensitive personal data in the questionnaire. Responses are stored locally in your browser unless you choose to submit them to your organization.

4) Research-Only Notice

This tool provides decision support and does not replace human review. All outputs require verification by a qualified reviewer.
